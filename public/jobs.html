Commission Only Sales Rep


[apply]


Find buyers, qualify them, close fixed-scope projects. You are selling real work to leaders, founders and directors at companies with actual problems worth solving. Not $99/mo SaaS, not AI slop solutions.


[what the job looks like]


Source your own pipeline. We support, you own it. Qualify hard against our ICP. Run discovery, scope the problem, handle the sales process. Hand off to delivery once signed.


[you are a fit if]


You have sold technical services or B2B software before, and you can tell the difference. You can hold a conversation with a prospect without leaning on buzzwords. You are comfortable disqualifying prospects who are not a fit. You want commission upside, not a salary floor.


[you are not a fit if]


You need leads handed to you. You sell on hype and worry about substance later. "Commission-only" reads as a red flag instead of an opportunity.


[compensation]


Commission-only. 22% of contract value on closed deals, paid on collection. Above the market average for this role. 6% recurring on retainer revenue you originate for the first 12 months, also above average. No cap.
